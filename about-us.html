Welcome to our Institute

ACU Law Students’ Society (‘ACU LSS’) is the peak representative body for Ajayi Crowther University Law Students. As the largest student-run organisation at ACU, the LSS aims to elevate the standard of university life for ACU Law Students to its utmost potential. We do this by meeting their educational, vocational and social needs. We aim to achieve this through a variety of activities, such as:

  • Hosting guest speakers and panel discussions to provide insights into different areas of law and the legal profession
  • Organizing networking events to connect law students with alumni and practicing attorneys
  • Providing mentorship and guidance to help law students succeed academically and professionally
  • Participating in community service and pro bono activities to give back to the community
