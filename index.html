Welcome to our Institute

Hello, welcome to the official website of the law student organisation of Ajayi Crowther University. We are glad to engage you throughout the session on the fascinating highlights of the Law College and the LSS body as a whole.

We are a dedicated group of students committed to enhancing the law school experience for all members of our community. We strive to provide our members with a wide range of opportunities to get involved and make an impact, both within the law school and in the larger legal community.

President's Address

My name is Sanu Oluwakamikun and I am the 2022/2023 President of the Ajayi Crowther University Law Students’ Society, otherwise known as ‘ACULSS’.

Formed in 2013, ACULSS has developed a strong foundation and now facilitates a range of events that aim to enrich the lives of the Law Students on the ACU Campus, Our initiatives cover several areas, such as competitions, professional development, well-being and social events.

In particular, we are proud to have fostered our reputation both internally and externally, through our strong connections with other Law Student Society. Throughout the year, all our publications, event information, links, and contact details will be made available to you here. I am incredibly passionate about everything ACULSS offers, and I hope that this webpage provides you with the resources to ensure your University experience is a positive one I encourage you to take a look around and bookmark the page so you can always come back!

Sincerely, Sanu Oluwakamikun
